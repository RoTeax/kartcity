Adult Track (16+)

Our Adult Track is 1km of smooth asphalt long and fully flood lit for night racing. Boasting a great mix of slow, medium and high speed turns which will challenge even the best drivers. The track is 7m wide which gives plenty of space to overtake your friends and prove once and for all who the best driver in your group is.


Our track has a full professional timing system used to track your kart in any given race so you can find out what your lap times are and how you improved throughout the session.
